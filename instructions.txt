
How to play RhymeIt

RhymeIt is a game designed to challenge your english vocabulary skills. You will be shown a word, to which you need to find a rhyme.
This is not always as easy as it sounds, some words rhyme phonetically and will be hard to think of. You will recieve a point for 
each correct rhyme. A false rhyme will result in Game Over. You can beat your high score, the sky's the limit. 
Have fun!
